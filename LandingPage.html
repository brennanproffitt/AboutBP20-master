Welcome!

About Me:

My name is Brennan Proffitt. I am currently an undergraduate student at Pittsburg State University and I am seeking a Bachelor of Business Administration with a major in Computer Information Systems. I have learned a lot during my time at PSU, and some of my favorite classes have involved developing mobile and web applications, as well as designing relational databases.


Work History:

Throughout my undergraduate program, I have been working at The Home Depot as a part-time associate. I spent three years as a sales associate for the lumber and millwork departments and gained lots of skills and knowledge when it comes to home improvement projects. While what I've learned as an associate is not directly related to my degree, I have been able to develop strong interpersonal and communication skills that are applicable to any career. I am still working with The Home Depot, but I am now on the Merchandising Execution Team. We are responsible for carrying out various projects and servicing merchandised bays to help sell and keep track of product. It is certainly a different aspect of the company, but I've loved it so far. Many of the projects require hands on use of tools as well as problem solving when issues occur.
